About ALK LABS

ALK LABS is an independent mobile app studio focused on creating high-quality puzzle and arcade experiences. We believe great games don't need to be complicated—they should be intuitive, engaging, and enjoyable from the very first tap.

Our mission is to blend thoughtful design, retro inspiration, and modern mobile experiences into games that players can enjoy anytime, anywhere.
