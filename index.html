About

ALK Lab is an independent app studio focused on creating fun, lightweight and accessible mobile experiences. From retro-inspired arcade games to brain-teasing puzzle challenges, every app is designed with simplicity, polish and enjoyment in mind.

Our goal is to build games that are easy to pick up, rewarding to play, and enjoyable for players of all ages.
